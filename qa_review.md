# Board Memo Draft

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
CareFlow Health continues to grow, but current operating signals indicate pressure in both demand generation and implementation capacity. ARR reached $23.2M, below the $23.9M plan. Pipeline coverage declined to 2.8x, and forecast accuracy fell to 86%. Implementation utilization remains at 93%, which is above the sustainable operating threshold.

## PERFORMANCE
ARR increased by $0.4M during the latest month but remains $0.7M below plan. Retention is 97.1%, still strong for the business, though it has declined for two consecutive months. The Northeast territory is the primary pipeline gap.

## RISKS
1. Insufficient pipeline creation may affect future bookings.
2. Sustained implementation utilization may delay customer launches.
3. Lower forecast accuracy may reduce confidence in resource decisions.

## RECOMMENDATIONS
- Rebalance enterprise prospecting capacity toward the Northeast.
- Add temporary implementation capacity and prioritize launches by revenue impact.
- Accelerate onboarding workflow automation.
- Apply stricter opportunity stage-exit criteria.

## DECISIONS REQUESTED
1. Approve temporary implementation contractor capacity.
2. Endorse a 30-day Northeast pipeline recovery plan.
3. Prioritize onboarding automation in the next product planning cycle.
